How to Play Buraco

Game Objective

Buraco is a card game played with 2 standard decks (104 cards + 4 jokers). The goal is to create card combinations (sequences or sets) and exhaust all cards in your hand.

Game Modes:

  • 2 Players: You vs Computer (classic)
  • 4 Players: Team 2 vs 2 - You + Partner (Computer 2) vs 2 opposing Computers

Card Values

  • Joker & 2: Wild cards - can replace any card
  • A (Ace): 15 points
  • K, Q, J, 10, 9, 8: 10 points
  • 7, 6, 5, 4, 3: 5 points

How to Play

  1. Game Start: Each player gets 11 cards. Each player also has their own "Pot" (11 hidden cards) that can be taken when cards run out.
  2. Your Turn:
    • Draw card: From deck OR take ENTIRE discard pile
    • Create combinations: Optional, minimum 3 cards
    • Discard 1 card to discard pile
  3. First Meld: Your first combination must be worth at least 50 points!

  5. Buraco (Clean Run): 7+ card combination without wild cards = 200 bonus points!
  6. Dirty Run: 7+ card combination with wild cards = 100 bonus points
  7. Pot: When your cards run out, your pot automatically opens and becomes your new hand

How to Win

Round Win Condition: To exhaust cards and end the round, you MUST have at least 1 Buraco (7+ card combination)!

Multi-Round Game: Game continues until a player reaches 2000 points. Each round:

  • Card combinations: +points according to card values
  • Buraco (clean run 7+ without wild): +200 bonus points
  • Dirty run (7+ with wild cards): +100 bonus points
  • Remaining cards in hand: -points (penalty)
  • Player who exhausts cards first: no penalty

Winner: Player with highest total score when someone reaches 2000 points!

Playing Tips

  • Top priority: Create at least 1 Buraco to be able to exhaust cards!
  • First meld: Collect cards until you can create 50+ point combinations
  • Take discard pile: Strategic because you take ALL cards, could be many!
  • Focus on creating long sequences to get clean Buraco (200 points)
  • Use Jokers and 2s strategically to complete combinations
  • Pay attention to cards discarded by opponents
  • Don't rush to exhaust cards if you don't have a Buraco yet
  • Discard high-value cards if they can't be combined
  • Pot will automatically open when cards run out - plan well!
